FAQ

What is Thrivetalk?

Thrivetalk is an online therapy platform designed to help you thrive in all areas of life. Our licensed and highly skilled therapists are available to assist with a wide range of concerns such as relationships, sex, PTSD, depression, social anxiety, or simply improving your self-care routine. We bring quality therapy right to your home.

Why should I choose ThriveTalk?

At ThriveTalk, we believe in making therapy accessible and comfortable. You can receive professional therapy from the comfort of your own couch through your laptop or mobile phone. If you're struggling to find the right therapist, ThriveTalk offers an easy and effective solution. Our licensed therapists provide the same high-quality care you'd receive in an office setting, but with the convenience of being wherever you are.

What issues can ThriveTalk help with?

Our therapists can support you with a wide variety of issues, including but not limited to:

  • Relationship issues

  • Sexual health concerns

  • PTSD (Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder)

  • Depression

  • Social anxiety

  • Self-care and personal development

How does ThriveTalk work?

Simply take our free online assessment to see if you’re a candidate for therapy with us. Once you’re ready, you can start your journey with a licensed therapist and begin improving your mental health and well-being right away.

Do you offer therapy for mental health conditions like depression or anxiety?

Yes, ThriveTalk offers support for a range of mental health conditions, including major depressive disorder (MDD), anxiety disorders, and more. MDD affects over 16.1 million American adults annually, and our goal is to provide support and treatment for those who need it.
